# SYS-001: Telemetry Data Rate

## Rationale

Real-time telemetry is essential for situational awareness during Beyond Visual Line of Sight (BVLOS) operations. A 10 Hz minimum rate ensures the ground operator can detect anomalies within 100ms.

[SYS]
The UAV system shall transmit telemetry data to the ground station at a minimum rate of 10 Hz under normal flight conditions.
